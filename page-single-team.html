Megan McMahon

UX Researcher

As a Senior UX Researcher at Mercari, my role involves leading generative research across multiple product teams, enhancing user experiences, and informing product development cycles. I draw on various methodologies, including interviews, benchmarking, diary studies, participatory workshops, concept explorations, surveys and usability testing. Collectively, our team's efforts have resulted in a 15% increase in inventory growth.

My educational foundation in Psychology from UT Austin, imparted me with rigorous training in both in-depth interviewing and advanced quantitative analyses, which I now leverage to power product discovery, identify UX improvements, and scope future research needs. Though I work remotely, you may bump into me at Ladies that UX Richmond or RVA Design Group.
