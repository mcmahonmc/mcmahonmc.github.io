Long Bio

Megan McMahon transforms customer understanding into strategic advantage. Starting her career in psychology—conducting research, running experiments, and mastering behavioral analysis during her PhD—she now applies this scientific rigor to solve complex business challenges.

Megan's expertise spans healthcare, higher education, and C2C e-commerce, where she's led research initiatives that have shaped product strategy and market positioning. She's particularly skilled at helping organizations build sustainable research practices that drive decision-making at every level.

Known for her ability to bridge analytical thinking with creative problem-solving, Megan facilitates workshops and innovation sprints that transform team insights into actionable strategy. She believes in the power of structured experimentation—designing targeted tests, learning through action, and iterating strategically to drive meaningful change.

When not leading research initiatives or facilitating workshops, she teaches future-thinking courses at Virginia Commonwealth University and spends her free time exploring the James River Park System.
