Medium Bio

Megan McMahon is a UX researcher and strategist who helps founders and teams uncover customer insights, test ideas, and build better products. With a PhD in experimental psychology, she applies behavioral science to design thinking, innovation, and business strategy. Her experience spans healthcare, higher education, and C2C e-commerce, where she’s led research, facilitated workshops, and helped teams make smarter, customer-driven decisions.

When she’s not running research or coaching founders, you’ll probably find her geeking out over behavioral science, designing new ways to teach innovation, or exploring the James River Park System.

Long Bio

Megan McMahon started her career in clinical psychology, studying human behavior, running experiments, and conducting hundreds of interviews. But instead of staying in academia, she applied her expertise in behavioral science to solve real-world business challenges.

Her career has taken her across industries—working in healthcare, higher education, and C2C e-commerce—to help companies and entrepreneurs uncover customer insights, test ideas, and build better products. She’s led research initiatives, facilitated team workshops, and helped organizations shift from assumptions to evidence-based decision-making.

Through Discovery Studio, Megan provides founders and teams with research-driven strategies, actionable tools, and hands-on coaching to navigate product-market fit, pricing, and growth. She believes in the power of experimentation—designing small tests, learning through action, and iterating quickly to drive meaningful change.

When she’s not deep in research or leading workshops, you can find her designing new ways to teach innovation, geeking out over behavioral science, or exploring the James River Park System.
