About Megan

With a foundation in cognitive psychology and a passion for design, I bring a research-driven approach to creating engaging learning experiences. Over the years, I’ve applied my expertise in UX research and behavioral science to projects that transform how people learn—whether by building user-centered tools or crafting educational programs that foster collaboration and growth.

Fueled by a belief that learning is most impactful when it’s hands-on and meaningful, I combine insights from neuroscience, design thinking, and instructional design to empower learners and organizations alike. I’m here to help make your learning initiatives both effective and inspiring.

Outside of the design world, I love playing with my rambunctious yellow lab, knitting, and hiking in national parks.

My Portfolio

I create impact by staying curious, prioritizing work that endures, and beginning with the end in mind.
All Design Strategy UX Research Instructional Design Workshops

  • Transforming Emergency Department Experiences for Rural Hospitals


    Using service design, systems thinking, and iterative testing to help patients feel more cared for and stay for treatment in rural emergency departments.

  • Secondhand Superstars


    A mixed-methods study drawing on a diary study, surveys, and user analytics to delve into the mental models and behavioral habits of a resale app's most engaged users.

  • Building Innovation Capacity at the Veterans Health Administration


    Creating a curriculum for the Veterans Health Administration Innovation Network, focusing on key methodologies such as design thinking, prototyping, storytelling, user testing, and remote user research. *Protected*

  • URL Listing


    Usability testing to understand design opportunities for a resale app's new feature enabling sellers to import listings from other resale platforms.

  • Reigniting Impact: A Design Thinking Workshop for AmeriCorps Virginia Housing Alliance


    This one-day workshop introduced design thinking and creativity methods to help recent college graduates develop practical solutions to address pressing challenges in affordable housing. *Protected*

  • Measuring Momentum


    Developing a UX benchmarking program powered by a self-service Looker dashboard, empowering teams to make data-driven decisions that drive product improvements.
