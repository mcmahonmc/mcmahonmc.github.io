Megan is a fourth year doctoral candidate in the Cognitive Neuroscience area. She has a MA in Clinical Psychology from UT Austin and a BS in Neuroscience from the College of William and Mary. Before coming to UT, Megan worked at Virginia Commonwealth University where she researched traumatic brain injury, cognitive outcomes in cancer patients, disability and stigma, and brain computer interfaces.
