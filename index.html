About Megan

With 8 years of research experience, I leverage my expertise in cognitive psychology to bridge the gap between human behavior and technology. Whether it's AI-driven products or emergency care, I combine empathy with rigorous, data-backed research to design solutions that truly resonate with users. My mission is to drive meaningful change that not only improves user experiences but also delivers tangible business results.

Outside of the product world, I love playing with my rambunctious yellow lab, hiking in national parks, knitting, and teaching.
